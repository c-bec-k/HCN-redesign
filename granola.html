All Real Food. End Of Story.

Delicious Noms Available for all Occasions!

Here at HippieChick Noms we believe in not only eating delicious and healthy food, but also in eating seasonally.

Because of this we have a few options available year-round but also flavors that are only available when their ingredients are in-season! So be sure to check with us often to see if your favorite nom is available!

Granola

Bananas for Coconuts

Our most snackable granola is a combination of dehydrated banana, coconut flakes and chia seeds, mixed with our three grain mix, mashed banana and coconut milk. This one has been known to disappear shortly after the bag is opened, without ever making it to a bowl. This is a must-have for anyone who is Bananas for Coconuts.

Ingredients: Bananas, oats†, millet†, buckwheat†, coconut flakes†, dried banana†, chia seeds†, coconut milk† (organic coconuts, water, organic guar gum).

†Certified Organic

Named after those great books you used to read as a kid, this granola lets you choose what you want in it. Our original flavor without nuts, seeds, or dried fruit and spices reminiscent of chai allows you to choose your granola adventure. The possibilities are endless!

Cranberries in My Apple Pie

With pumpkin spice everywhere, poor apple pie gets left behind. But not around here. We're obsessed with apples. That's why this fall seasonal flavor is made with locally grown apples and baked apples pieces. Dried cranberries and apple pie spices are in the mix to warm you up on cool, fall days. Now you can have apple pie anytime.

Ingredients: Apple Purée (apples, water), oats†, millet†, buckwheat†, cranberries (apple juice concentrate, canola and/or sunflower oil [less than 1\%]), dried apples†, cinnamon†, nutmeg†, allspice†.

†Certified Organic

When the snow starts to fall, we know it's time to warm up with some peppermint hot chocolate. Now you can have it without the trip to the coffee shop. Made with rich cocoa powder and peppermint almonds, this winter seasonal flavor is perfect for any time of day!
