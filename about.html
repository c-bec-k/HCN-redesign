About HippieChick Noms

Life is too short to eat bad food.

Rachel has always been in love with breakfast foods. After being fed up with a variety of health problems, she turned to food for the solution. She learned what did and didn't work for her. Sugar and oil were out, which meant so was granola. Then one day Christopher said, "you can probably make it without." Using her food and nutrition knowledge she did and it was amazing! They decided they couldn't keep it all to themselves and HippieChick Noms was born. Now you can enjoy our love for breakfast (or anytime) granola!

Meet The Hippies

The HippieChick and Granola Maker

Rachel is the (not-so-)evil mastermind behind HippieChick Noms. Not only does she make the granola but she gives the company its name: she is The HippieChick!

Business Manager and Taste-Tester

Christopher is the nerd of the company who likes his spreadsheets and numbers. Using his head for figures, he is the business manager for HippieChick Noms and manages the back-end of things. When possible he does like to attend markets and events to meet people.
